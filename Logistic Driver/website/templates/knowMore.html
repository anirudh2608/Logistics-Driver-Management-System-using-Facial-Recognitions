Logistics' Driver Management System


This is a logistics driver management system developed by Arjun Bajaj. The project main aim is to provide a secure and safe road journey of a truck driver as many cases are arrising in India related to truck drivers illegally driving vehicles. The security aspect of the project is that the face identity of the accepted/hired drivers is in the database and everytime a driver need to start/stop/pause his journey, he needs to verify his identity. Without it, he/she cannot even see their Dashboard. Hope you like the project.
